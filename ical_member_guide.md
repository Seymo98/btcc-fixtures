# BTCC Fixture Calendar — How to Subscribe

**Get all your team's fixtures in your phone calendar — automatically updated.**

No more checking WhatsApp or the website for fixture times. Subscribe once and every match appears in your calendar. When fixtures change or results come in, your calendar updates automatically.

---

## Choose Your Feed

| Feed | What's Included |
|------|----------------|
| **1st XI** | All 1st XI league, cup and friendly matches |
| **2nd XI** | All 2nd XI league, cup and friendly matches |
| **Sunday XI** | Sunday league and friendly matches |
| **Midweek XI** | Midweek T20 matches |
| **All Seniors** | Everything above in one feed |
| **Juniors** | All junior age group matches |

---

## iPhone / iPad

1. Your captain or team admin will send you a **link** in the WhatsApp group
2. Tap the link — it will open a prompt saying **"Subscribe to calendar?"**
3. Tap **Subscribe**
4. Done! Fixtures appear in your Calendar app

To check it's working: open **Settings → Calendar → Accounts** — you should see the BTCC feed listed.

Your calendar will refresh automatically (usually every few hours).

---

## Android / Google Calendar

1. Your captain or team admin will send you a **link** in the WhatsApp group
2. Open the link in Chrome — it may ask you to add to Google Calendar
3. If it doesn't open automatically:
   - Open **Google Calendar** on your phone
   - Tap the **☰ menu** → **Settings** → **Add calendar** → **From URL**
   - Paste the link your captain sent
4. Done! Fixtures appear in your Google Calendar

---

## Google Calendar (Desktop)

1. Open [calendar.google.com](https://calendar.google.com)
2. On the left sidebar, find **Other calendars** and click the **+**
3. Select **From URL**
4. Paste the feed link
5. Click **Add calendar**

The calendar will sync every 12–24 hours.

---

## Outlook

1. Open Outlook Calendar
2. Click **Add calendar** → **Subscribe from web**
3. Paste the feed link
4. Give it a name (e.g., "BTCC 1st XI")
5. Click **Import**

---

## What You'll See

Each fixture shows:
- **Match title:** BTCC 1st XI vs Sileby Town CC (H)
- **Time:** Start time with estimated finish
- **Location:** Ground name (tap for directions)
- **Details:** Competition name

After a match is played, the event updates with the result:
- **BTCC 1st XI vs Sileby Town CC (H) — BTCC won by 36 runs**
